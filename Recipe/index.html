Homemade Pizza

Prep Time: 30 mins | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings (pepperoni, olives, etc.)

Instructions

  • Mix flour, yeast, and water to make dough.
  • Knead dough for 5 minutes and let rise for 20 minutes.
  • Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  • Roll out dough and add sauce and toppings.
  • Bake for 15-20 minutes until crust is golden.
